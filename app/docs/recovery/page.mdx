# Recovery

How to recover your wallet if you lose access.

## Recovery Code

When you create your wallet, you receive a **recovery code**. This is the third Shamir share. Combined with the server share, it meets the 2-of-3 threshold to reconstruct your key.

> **Keep your recovery code safe.** Store it offline in a secure location. Without it, wallet recovery may not be possible.

## Recovery Scenarios

### I forgot my PIN

1. Go to **Settings > Reset PIN**
2. Enter your **recovery code**
3. The server share + recovery code reconstruct your key
4. Set a new PIN
5. Your device share is re-encrypted with the new PIN

> After 5 failed PIN attempts, your wallet locks for 15 minutes as a security measure.

### I lost my device

1. Open **@ccbotwallet_bot** on a new device
2. Select **Recover Wallet**
3. Enter your **recovery code**
4. The recovery code + server share reconstruct your key
5. Set a new PIN on the new device
6. Your wallet is restored with full access

### I lost my recovery code (but have my device)

If you still have access to your device and PIN, your wallet continues to work normally. The device share + server share are sufficient for all operations.

However, you should **generate a new recovery code** as soon as possible from Settings, in case you lose your device in the future.

## Email-Based Recovery

If you have verified your email address with CC Bot Wallet:

1. Select **Recover via Email**
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Enter the 6-digit verification code sent to your email
4. Complete the recovery process

## Important Notes

- **We cannot recover your wallet** without your recovery code or verified email
- **Recovery codes are one-time use** - after recovery, a new code is generated
- **All recovery attempts are logged** in the security audit trail
