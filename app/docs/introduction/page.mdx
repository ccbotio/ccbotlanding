# What is CC Bot Wallet?

CC Bot Wallet is a **self-custodial cryptocurrency wallet** built on the Canton Network, accessible directly through Telegram as a Mini App.

It lets you send, receive, swap, and bridge digital assets without ever leaving Telegram. Your private keys are split using **2-of-3 Shamir Secret Sharing**, meaning no single party - including us - can access your funds.

## Key Features

- **Self-Custodial** - You control your keys. We only hold one encrypted share.
- **Telegram Native** - Full wallet experience inside Telegram. No app download needed.
- **Canton Network** - Built on an institutional-grade blockchain with sub-second finality.
- **Token Swap** - Swap CC and USDCx instantly within the wallet.
- **Multi-Chain** - Built-in EVM support with Ethereum address derived from the same seed. No external wallet needed.
- **Cross-Chain Bridge** - Bridge USDC from Ethereum to Canton via Circle xReserve, directly from the app.
- **AI Agent** - Manage your wallet through natural language conversation.

## How It Works

CC Bot Wallet operates as a Telegram Mini App. When you open the bot, you interact with a full-featured wallet interface powered by the Canton Network.

Your Ed25519 private key is generated on-device and immediately split into three shares:

1. **Device share** - Encrypted with your PIN, stored locally on your device
2. **Server share** - Encrypted with AES-256-GCM, stored on our server
3. **Recovery code** - Given to you at wallet creation for backup

Any **two of three** shares can reconstruct your key. This means you can always access your funds, and we can never access them alone.
